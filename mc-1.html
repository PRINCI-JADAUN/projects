About AIIMS Delhi

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi is one of the leading medical institutions in India, offering world-class education, modern infrastructure, and excellent research opportunities. It provides undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines of medicine and healthcare.
