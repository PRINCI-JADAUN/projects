About Krishna Institute

Krishna Institute of Technology and Engineering (KITE) is one of the leading engineering institutions in the NCR region, offering world-class education, modern infrastructure, and excellent placement opportunities. It provides undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines of engineering and management.
