About IIT Delhi

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi is one of the premier engineering institutions in India, offering world-class education, modern infrastructure, and excellent placement opportunities. It provides undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines of engineering and management.
